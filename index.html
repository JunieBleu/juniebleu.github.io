About Me
I am a passionate Artist and Sewist.

I am a passionate Artist and Sewist, I have been working creativly since I was young and have always had an affinity for the arts.

In my free time I am a costume maker and digital illustator focasing on Anime charactors and Book charactors.
I have even competed with my costumes in the World Cosplay Summit Australia Preliminary Competition!

Artist

I have been a practising Artist most of my life, experimenting with a wide variety of medium and techniques. My current favourite mediums and specilties include: Digital Illustration, Photography and Video Design.

Sewist

I have 10 years of sewing experience. I am capable in Patterning, Mock-ups, Cutting, Sewing and Finishings of garments. I however, specialize in Costumes and the fun techniques encompased (such as quick change costumes) .

Teacher

Being passionate in so many creative areas has lead me to Teaching, where I am able to share my creative world with my Students and show them that Art is (1) Useful (2) Valuble & (3) Timeless.
